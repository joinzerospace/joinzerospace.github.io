About Zero Space

Zero Space thrusts you into a vast, procedurally generated universe teeming with danger and opportunity. Take command of your custom starship, engage in thrilling combat, explore uncharted systems, build formidable bases, and forge alliances (or rivalries) in a dynamic multiplayer sandbox.

Key Features:

  • Fast-paced PvP and PvE space combat
  • Explore infinitely generated galaxies
  • Deep ship customization & upgrade system
  • Form guilds, join factions, dominate the economy
  • Strategic base building and resource management

The Universe of Zero Space

In the aftermath of the Great Collapse, humanity scattered across the stars. Now, centuries later, new factions rise, ancient technologies resurface, and the silent void known as Zero Space holds secrets both wondrous and terrifying. Discover the history, factions, and mysteries that shape your journey.

Factions

Learn about the major powers vying for control: the technologically advanced Cygnus Combine, the resource-hungry Orion Syndicate, the enigmatic Void Walkers, and the independent Free Colonies.

History: The Great Collapse

Uncover the events that led to the fall of the Terran Federation and the subsequent diaspora into the unknown reaches of Zero Space.

Alien Species & Anomalies

Not all life in Zero Space is human. Encounter strange alien species, navigate cosmic phenomena, and explore remnants of precursor civilizations.
